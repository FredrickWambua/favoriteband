About Imagine Dragons

Formed in 2009, Imagine Dragons first revealed their limitless passion and genre-bending sensibilities with a series of independently released EPs that quickly earned a grassroots following. Over the last half-decade, Imagine Dragons have emerged as one of the biggest and most compelling rock bands in the world. Within a year of its June 2017 release, their third studio album EVOLVE went double-platinum, yielding three back-to-back #1 singles: Believer, Thunder, and It Takes. Self-identifying as “genreless,” Imagine Dragon artfully blends rock, hip-hop, pop, and folk with their lyrics that speak to a sometimes-painful self-awareness, but ultimately uplift and inspire.

Members

Dan Reynold:Singer.

He is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is the lead vocalist and only remaining original member of the Imagine Dragons band. He is a recipient of the Songwriters Hall of Fame Hal David Starlight Award.

Wayne Sermon:Guitarist.

He is an American musician, songwriter, and record producer. He is the lead guitarist for the Imagine Dragons band. As a youth Sermon learned to play both cello and guitar. He was determined to be a guitarist even as a child. His dad had an audio-phile quality amplifier, a record player, and all of The Beatles albums on vinyl, which Sermon enjoyed listening to.

Ben Mkcee:Bass.

He is an American musician, songwriter, and record producer. He is the bassist for the pop rock band Imagine Dragons. He grew up playing acoustic guitar and violin, before picking up acoustic bass in fifth grade. In high school, he continued learning bass guitar as a member of a jazz trio, which influenced his decision to attend Berklee College of Music.

Daniel Platzman:Drumist.

He is an American musician, songwriter, record producer and composer. He is the drummer for the Imagine Dragons band. He attended Berklee College of Music where he earned a degree in film scoring. While at Berklee, Platzman played in the Concert Jazz Orchestra, the Urban Outreach Jazz Orchestra and the Berklee Rainbow Big Band, and received the Vic Firth Award for Outstanding Musicianship
